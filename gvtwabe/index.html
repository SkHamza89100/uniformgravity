About Us

Gravity Uniforms — Excellence Since 2009

Since 2009, Gravity Uniforms has been a leading manufacturer and supplier of high-quality uniforms for schools, corporate houses, hospitals, and industries across India. Based in Kolkata, we combine craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver uniforms that represent your institution with pride.

Our state-of-the-art production facility ensures every piece meets the highest quality standards, from fabric selection to final stitching.

  • Fully customized uniforms with your logo & colors
  • Bulk manufacturing capacity for schools & corporates
  • Premium fabrics — breathable, durable & washable
  • Delivery across all 28 states of India
  • Government school uniform supplier (WB, Assam, Odisha)
